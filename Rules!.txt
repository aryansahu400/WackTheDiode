1) Each player gets 30 secs to play.

2) The player should move the joystick in the direction of the last glowing led.

3) For a correct hit, the player gets a point.

4) If any player misses more than 7 times, the game is over and all the leds will glow.

5) The score will be displayed on the Binary Score board, where a glowing led represents 1 and turned off led represents 0.
